Next week's plans:

opeo-maven-plugin:

  • To remove the crutch in FormattingDecompiler.java (lines 113-118) and fix the problem [#358]

Risks:

eo:

  • The XMIR format is missing an anonymous abstract object for bytes, potentially causing compilation issues [#3807].

jeo-maven-plugin:

  • The 'xmir-to-phi' transformation is time-consuming; we might experience slow build times [#633].
  • The use of parallelThreads in Maven plugins causes flaky behavior in the build process [#1044].
  • Bytecode verification cannot be enabled for the 'spring-fat' integration test, which may lead to undetected issues [#543].
